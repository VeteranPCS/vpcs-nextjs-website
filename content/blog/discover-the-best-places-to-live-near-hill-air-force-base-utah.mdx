For military families and civilian employees relocating to **Hill Air Force Base (HAFB)**, finding the right community to call home is an essential part of the transition. Located in **Davis County, Utah**, Hill AFB is surrounded by a mix of vibrant cities and quiet suburban towns, each offering unique benefits based on lifestyle preferences, commute time, and family needs.

Whether you are looking for **affordability, top-rated schools, easy base access, or outdoor recreation**, the communities near Hill AFB provide a variety of options to fit your priorities. This guide explores some of the **best places to live near Hill Air Force Base**, helping you make an informed decision for your next move.

## **Hill Air Force Base: A Strong Military Community**

As **one of the largest and most critical Air Force installations in the United States**, Hill AFB plays a key role in national defense and regional economic growth. Its location in **northern Utah** provides a **high quality of life** for military families, with strong community support, outdoor activities, and a relatively low cost of living compared to other military bases.

The surrounding cities each offer **distinct advantages**, from bustling urban centers to quiet suburban retreats. Here’s a look at **four of the most popular areas for military families** near Hill AFB.

## **Top 4 Cities Near Hill AFB for Military Families**

### **1. Layton: Urban Convenience with Outdoor Beauty**

Layton is the **closest city to Hill AFB**, making it an ideal location for service members who want to minimize their commute. The city is known for **its mix of suburban and urban amenities**, offering a balance between convenience and outdoor access.

**What Makes Layton a Great Choice?**

- **Commute:** Just minutes from Hill AFB, reducing time spent in traffic.
- **Shopping & Dining:** Home to **Layton Hills Mall**, movie theaters, and a variety of restaurants.
- **Outdoor Activities:** Close to **Antelope Island State Park, the Great Salt Lake, and the Wasatch Mountains**, making it perfect for hiking, biking, and fishing.
- **Education:** Offers well-rated schools and **Weber State University’s Layton campus**.

**Who is Layton Best For?**
Military families who **value convenience, city amenities, and outdoor access** will find Layton a great fit.

### **2. Clearfield: Affordable and Family-Friendly**

Clearfield provides a **cost-effective living option** for those looking to stay close to Hill AFB. The area offers a **mix of single-family homes and apartment complexes**, making it attractive for a wide range of homebuyers and renters.

**What Makes Clearfield a Great Choice?**

- **Affordability:** Home prices and rental costs are lower compared to nearby Layton and Farmington.
- **Community Amenities:** Offers **parks, libraries, and community events** that promote a family-friendly environment.
- **Local Attractions:** Home to **Hill Aerospace Museum**, which is a favorite among military families and aviation enthusiasts.
- **Schools:** The **Davis School District** serves the area with several well-regarded public schools.

**Who is Clearfield Best For?**
Military families and first-time homebuyers looking for an **affordable, community-driven environment with easy base access**.

### **3. Clinton: Small-Town Charm with Modern Comforts**

Clinton is known for **its quiet suburban feel, strong sense of community, and slower pace of life** compared to busier cities like Layton. It offers **a mix of new developments and established neighborhoods**, making it a great place for families looking for more space and a welcoming environment.

**What Makes Clinton a Great Choice?**

- **Quiet Atmosphere:** Less traffic and noise, ideal for families looking for a peaceful setting.
- **Outdoor Space:** Multiple parks and **sports fields**, perfect for children and pets.
- **Community Events:** The **annual Heritage Days Festival** brings residents together for celebrations and local traditions.
- **Cost of Living:** Offers a lower cost of living compared to Farmington while still being close to major amenities.

**Who is Clinton Best For?**
Military families who prefer **a slower-paced, family-focused community with easy access to Hill AFB**.

### **4. Farmington: A Blend of Tradition and Modernity**

Farmington offers **a mix of historic charm and modern developments**, making it one of the most desirable locations near Hill AFB. While home prices are higher than in Layton or Clearfield, the city provides **excellent schools, premier shopping, and great recreational activities**.

**What Makes Farmington a Great Choice?**

- **Station Park:** A **high-end shopping, dining, and entertainment center** that attracts families and professionals.
- **Education:** Home to some of **the top-rated schools** in Davis County.
- **Lagoon Amusement Park:** A well-known **family-friendly attraction** located in the heart of Farmington.
- **Easy Commute:** Access to **Interstate 15 and the FrontRunner commuter train** makes it easy to reach Hill AFB or downtown Salt Lake City.

**Who is Farmington Best For?**
Military families **seeking excellent schools, premium amenities, and a balance of modern and historic living**.

## **Choosing the Right Community for Your PCS Move**

Each of these cities near Hill AFB **offers something unique** for military families, whether it’s **affordability, proximity to base, family-friendly communities, or modern conveniences**. Finding the right fit depends on your specific needs, such as:

- **Commute time** – Layton is the closest, while Farmington offers easy highway access.
- **Housing budget** – Clearfield and Clinton are more affordable, while Farmington is higher-end.
- **Community environment** – Clinton is quiet and suburban, while Layton has more urban amenities.
- **Schools and family amenities** – Farmington and Layton offer top-rated schools and parks.

No matter which location you choose, you can find **a supportive community near Hill AFB that understands the unique needs of military families**.

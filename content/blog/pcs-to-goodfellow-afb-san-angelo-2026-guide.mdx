If you have orders for a PCS to Goodfellow Air Force Base, welcome to San Angelo, Texas. PCS stands for Permanent Change of Station, the military term for moving from one duty station to another. Goodfellow is a compact, mission-focused installation in the heart of the Concho Valley, and it draws students and permanent-party personnel from every branch of the military. This 2026 guide will walk you through what to expect when you arrive: where to live, how housing pay works, schools, healthcare, the local climate, and a few things to do once you are settled.

A PCS move is a lot to manage. Our goal is to make the San Angelo part feel a little smaller and a lot clearer.

## What and Where Is Goodfellow Air Force Base?

Goodfellow Air Force Base is located in San Angelo, in Tom Green County in west-central Texas. It sits on the southern edge of the city, roughly two miles from downtown San Angelo.

The installation is home to the [17th Training Wing (17 TRW)](https://www.goodfellow.af.mil/About-Us/), which falls under Air Education and Training Command, or AETC. According to the official Goodfellow AFB website, the 17 TRW mission is to "Train, Transform, and Empower joint and coalition warriors." That mission runs on two pillars.

The first is intelligence, surveillance, and reconnaissance, or ISR, training. The [17th Training Group](https://www.goodfellow.af.mil/Units/17th-Training-Group/) trains ISR warriors at initial, intermediate, and advanced levels, graduating nearly 14,000 students each year who go on to support combat units and national-level agencies worldwide.

The second is fire protection. Goodfellow hosts the DoD's centralized fire-protection training program, producing fire professionals for all services.

What makes Goodfellow distinctive is its joint character. Army, Marine Corps, and Navy tenant units are all permanently present on the installation. The [344th Military Intelligence Battalion](https://www.goodfellow.af.mil/Units/Army-344th-Military-Intelligence-Battalion/), a [Marine Corps Detachment](https://www.goodfellow.af.mil/Units/Marine-Corps-Detachment/), and a [Naval Aviation Technical Training Command Learning Site](https://www.goodfellow.af.mil/Units/Naval-Aviation-Technical-Training-Command-Learning-Site-Goodfellow/) train here alongside Air Force students every day.

## Where to Live When PCSing to Goodfellow AFB

Most families who come to Goodfellow either live on post or settle in one of the surrounding communities in and around San Angelo. Because the base is inside the city, commutes from most off-post neighborhoods are short, and you have a real range of options at different price points.

### San Angelo

San Angelo is the city Goodfellow calls home. Most service members choose to live in town, with a commute of about 10 minutes or less. The city has established neighborhoods, newer subdivisions, apartments, and rentals across a wide price range. You are close to shopping, restaurants, and medical care, and the Concho River Walk is often within easy reach. It is the most practical choice for students and permanent-party personnel alike.

### Grape Creek

Grape Creek is a small community northwest of San Angelo, about 15 minutes from the base. It has a quieter, residential feel with easy access to the North Concho River. Families who want a little more space while keeping a reasonable commute often look here.

### Wall

Wall is a rural community about 20 minutes southwest of the base on the open prairie. It is home to Wall ISD, which draws families who want a smaller school district. Housing tends to offer larger lots, with the trade-off of a longer daily drive.

### Christoval

Christoval sits about 25 minutes south, along the South Concho River near the Hill Country edge. It is a quiet small town known for fishing and camping. Families who want a rural setting and do not mind limited local amenities sometimes land here.

## Goodfellow AFB BAH and the Cost of Living

If you live off post, the military helps cover your housing costs through BAH, which stands for Basic Allowance for Housing. BAH is a monthly, tax-free payment. The amount depends on three things: your rank, whether you have dependents, and your duty station's Military Housing Area, or MHA. An MHA is the geographic zone the Department of Defense uses to set local housing pay. Goodfellow AFB falls within the San Angelo, Texas MHA.

Because BAH rates change each year and vary by rank and dependent status, we do not publish a rate table here. To find your exact figure, use the official DoD calculator: [travel.dod.mil BAH Rate Lookup](https://www.travel.dod.mil/Allowances/Basic-Allowance-for-Housing/BAH-Rate-Lookup/). Enter your rank and ZIP code for the current rate. Always confirm your rate before signing a lease or making an offer.

San Angelo is one of the more affordable housing markets in Texas. The typical home value in San Angelo was about $221,396 as of April 2026, according to [Zillow](https://www.zillow.com/home-values/54294/san-angelo-tx/). That is a typical value, not a median sale price. As of the week of June 18, 2026, [Freddie Mac](https://www.freddiemac.com/pmms) reported the average 30-year fixed mortgage rate at 6.47 percent. Whether buying or renting makes more sense depends on how long you expect to be stationed here, so run the numbers for your situation.

## Schools Near Goodfellow AFB

The schools your children attend depend entirely on where you live. San Angelo and the surrounding area are served by several separate districts.

[San Angelo Independent School District](https://www.saisd.org/schools) is the largest local system and serves most families who live in the city. According to [NCES](https://nces.ed.gov/ccd/districtsearch/district_detail.asp?ID2=4838700), San Angelo ISD operates 21 schools serving grades pre-kindergarten through 12th grade, with enrollment concentrated in the city of San Angelo. The district includes Central High School, Lake View High School, and several middle and elementary schools across the city.

Families who live west of the city in the Wall area are served by Wall ISD, a smaller rural district that draws families who prefer a tighter-knit school community. Those who settle farther out toward Christoval fall under Christoval ISD.

Boundaries can shift, so confirm the assigned school for any specific address before you commit.

## Healthcare: Goodfellow Medical Group

On-base medical care is provided by the [Goodfellow Medical Group](https://goodfellow.tricare.mil/), which serves TRICARE-enrolled patients at the installation. The medical group offers primary care, dental, pharmacy, physical therapy, immunizations, and optometry, according to the [Goodfellow TRICARE site](https://goodfellow.tricare.mil/).

Your military health coverage runs through TRICARE, the health care program for service members and their families. Set up your plan early, update your information in DEERS, and arrange any referrals through the [Appointments and Referrals](https://goodfellow.tricare.mil/Getting-Care/Appointments-Referrals) page before you arrive so you have no gap in care.

## Climate and Things to Do in San Angelo

San Angelo sits in the Concho Valley, and the [National Weather Service in San Angelo](https://www.weather.gov/sjt/) classifies the region as semi-arid. Summers are hot and dry, often reaching the upper 90s, with lower humidity than most of Texas. Winters are mild, with occasional cold fronts and rare ice. Spring brings wind and some chance of severe weather. The big skies and dry air will feel noticeably different if you are arriving from a humid posting.

San Angelo offers more outdoor recreation than its size suggests.

[San Angelo State Park](https://tpwd.texas.gov/state-parks/san-angelo), managed by the Texas Parks and Wildlife Department, offers 50 miles of multiuse trails for hiking, mountain biking, and horseback riding, plus fishing, swimming, and camping. Families with kids will want to walk the Dinosaur Trail, which passes fossilized Permian-Age trackways. Bison and longhorn cattle at the park's paddocks are a bonus.

The Concho River Walk winds through downtown along the North and South Concho Rivers, with parks, public art, and local restaurants within easy walking distance.

[Fort Concho National Historic Landmark](https://www.sanangelo.gov/165/Fort-Concho) is one of the best-preserved frontier forts in the American West. Established in 1867 and listed on the National Register of Historic Places, it encompasses 24 original and restored structures in the heart of the city.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What MHA or area is Goodfellow AFB in for BAH?

Goodfellow AFB falls within the San Angelo, Texas Military Housing Area for BAH purposes. Your rate depends on your rank and whether you have dependents. Look up your exact figure with the official [DoD BAH calculator](https://www.travel.dod.mil/Allowances/Basic-Allowance-for-Housing/BAH-Rate-Lookup/) using your rank and ZIP code.

### Where do military families live near Goodfellow AFB?

The most common choice is San Angelo itself, which offers a 10-minute or shorter commute, a range of housing types, and full city amenities. Families who want a quieter setting often look at Grape Creek to the northwest, Wall to the southwest, or Christoval to the south. On-post housing is also available, so ask your sponsor and the housing office about current options and wait times.

### Is Goodfellow AFB a joint base?

Goodfellow is not officially designated a joint base, but it functions as one. The Army's 344th Military Intelligence Battalion, a Marine Corps Detachment, and a Navy training site are all permanently present. Students from every branch train side by side in ISR and fire protection courses.

### What school district will my children attend near Goodfellow AFB?

It depends on your address. Families in the city of San Angelo fall under San Angelo ISD, which operates 21 schools serving pre-kindergarten through grade 12. Families in the Wall area attend Wall ISD, and those near Christoval attend Christoval ISD. Confirm the assigned school for any specific home before you commit.

### Where do I get healthcare at Goodfellow AFB?

On-base care is provided by the [Goodfellow Medical Group](https://goodfellow.tricare.mil/), which offers primary care, dental, pharmacy, and other services for TRICARE-enrolled patients. Set up your TRICARE plan, update DEERS, and arrange referrals before you arrive to avoid any gap in coverage.

### What is the weather like in San Angelo, Texas?

San Angelo has a semi-arid climate. Summers are hot and dry, winters are mild with occasional cold fronts, and spring brings some wind and the chance of severe weather. The [National Weather Service in San Angelo](https://www.weather.gov/sjt/) has local forecasts and climate normals if you want to check month-by-month averages before your move.

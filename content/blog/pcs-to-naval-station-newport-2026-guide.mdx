If you have orders for a PCS to Naval Station Newport, welcome to one of the Navy's most distinctive duty stations. PCS stands for Permanent Change of Station, the military term for a government-directed move from one installation to another. Newport, Rhode Island sits on the southern tip of Aquidneck Island, surrounded by Narragansett Bay. It is a small, historic city with a large naval footprint and a setting that surprises a lot of incoming families.

This 2026 guide covers what NAVSTA Newport does, where families tend to live, how housing pay works, schools, healthcare, and what there is to do once you settle in.

## What and Where Is Naval Station Newport?

Naval Station Newport, or NAVSTA Newport, sits on the southwestern coast of Aquidneck Island in Newport County, Rhode Island. According to the [NAVSTA Newport About page](https://cnrma.cnic.navy.mil/Installations/NAVSTA-Newport/About/), the installation supports more than 10,000 military members, Department of Defense civilians, contractors, and family members who live, work, study, and train there. NAVSTA Newport is the Navy's primary hub for officer education and training on the East Coast, home to more than 40 naval and defense commands and activities.

The [U.S. Naval War College](https://www.usnwc.edu/) is the anchor institution. Founded on October 6, 1884, it is the oldest war college in continuous existence anywhere in the world. Each year, outstanding mid-level and senior officers from the Navy, other U.S. military services, federal civilian agencies, and international partner nations complete graduate-level study at NWC. The college awards Joint Professional Military Education, or JPME, credit and either a diploma or a master's degree.

Officer Training Command Newport, or OTCN, is the largest officer accessions point in the Navy, training approximately 1,500 students and candidates each year. According to the [CNIC tenant commands page](https://cnrma.cnic.navy.mil/Installations/NAVSTA-Newport/About/Tenant-Commands/Officer-Training-Command/), OTCN hosts four programs: Officer Candidate School, or OCS; Officer Development School; the Limited Duty Officer and Warrant Officer program; and the Naval Science Institute.

Also on station is the [Naval Undersea Warfare Center Division Newport](https://cnrma.cnic.navy.mil/Installations/NAVSTA-Newport/About/Tenant-Commands/Naval-Undersea-Warfare-Center-HQ-Div-Npt/), or NUWC Division Newport, the Navy's corporate laboratory for undersea warfare research and development. The Surface Warfare Officers School Command, or SWOSCOLCOM, trains surface warfare officers for fleet assignments. Additional tenant commands include the Naval Academy Preparatory School, the Senior Enlisted Academy, and the Naval Justice School.

## Where to Live When PCSing to Naval Station Newport

Most families choose between on-station housing and a community on or near Aquidneck Island. Your decision usually comes down to commute, schools, budget, and the setting you want. Newport is a walkable historic city, but housing inside the city limits is expensive. The towns north on the island and across the bridges offer more variety.

*Approximate one-way drive times to Naval Station Newport. Times vary with traffic and which gate you use.*

### Newport

Newport surrounds the station. Living in town means a five-minute commute, walkable access to the waterfront, and proximity to historic neighborhoods and the Cliff Walk. The trade-off is cost: Newport is one of the most expensive markets in Rhode Island and rental inventory is limited. Families who want to walk to base and the city often start here and adjust budget expectations.

### Middletown

Middletown borders Newport to the north and is the most popular off-station choice for military families. Drive times run about 10 minutes. You will find single-family homes, townhouses, and apartments at prices noticeably below Newport. Middletown has grocery stores, parks, and Easton's Beach. For most families, it hits the best balance of commute, cost, and livability.

### Portsmouth

Portsmouth is the next town north, about 15 minutes from the station. It is quieter and more suburban, with larger lots and a mix of older and newer construction. Families who want more space and a slightly lower price point often land here.

### Jamestown

Jamestown sits on Conanicut Island just across the Pell Bridge, about 15 minutes by car. It is a small island community with a waterfront feel and limited inventory. The Pell Bridge requires a toll, so factor that into your budget.

### Tiverton

Tiverton is on the eastern mainland, about 25 minutes from the station. It is more rural, with farmland and river views, and housing tends to cost less than on Aquidneck Island.

### Bristol

Bristol is a historic town connected by bridge to the north end of Aquidneck Island, roughly 30 minutes away. It offers community character and lower prices than Newport or Middletown for families who can absorb the longer drive.

## Naval Station Newport BAH and the Cost of Living

If you live off station, the military helps cover housing through BAH, which stands for Basic Allowance for Housing. BAH is a monthly, tax-free payment based on three things: your pay grade, whether you have dependents, and your duty station's Military Housing Area, or MHA. NAVSTA Newport falls within the Newport, Rhode Island MHA.

Because rates change each year and vary by rank and dependent status, we do not publish a rate table. To find your exact figure, use the official DoD calculator at [travel.dod.mil](https://www.travel.dod.mil/Allowances/Basic-Allowance-for-Housing/BAH-Rate-Lookup/). Enter your pay grade and ZIP code. Confirm your rate before signing a lease or making an offer.

For a sense of market costs, the typical home value in Newport was about $945,429 in April 2026, according to [Zillow](https://www.zillow.com/home-values/26138/newport-ri/), a typical value, not a median sale price. In Middletown, the typical home value was about $761,300 in April 2026, according to [Zillow](https://www.zillow.com/home-values/395591/middletown-ri/), also a typical value, not a median sale price. As of the week of June 18, 2026, [Freddie Mac](https://www.freddiemac.com/pmms) reported the average 30-year fixed mortgage rate at 6.47 percent.

Newport County is one of the pricier markets in New England, so running the numbers for your situation matters. ## Schools Near Naval Station Newport

The schools your children attend depend on where you live.

[Newport Public Schools](https://www.npsri.net/) serves the city of Newport and includes Claiborne Pell Elementary School, Frank E. Thompson Middle School, and William S. Rogers High School. The district also operates the Newport Area Career and Technical Center, a regional school serving students from Newport, Middletown, Portsmouth, Tiverton, and Little Compton.

[Middletown Public Schools](https://www.mpsri.net/) operates five schools serving approximately 1,904 students, according to the district's own data. Portsmouth has its own district serving the north end of Aquidneck Island, and Tiverton School District covers the eastern mainland. Boundaries can shift, so confirm the assigned school for any specific address before you commit.

## Healthcare: Naval Health Clinic New England, Newport

On-station medical care is provided by the [Naval Health Clinic New England, Newport](https://www.tricare.mil/GettingCare/FindDoctor/MTF/Facilities/Naval-Health-Clinic-New-England-Newport-RI), located at 43 Smith Road on Naval Station Newport. The clinic is open Monday through Friday from 7:30 a.m. to 6 p.m., with the pharmacy open Saturdays from 8 a.m. to noon. Appointments are made through the patient portal.

Your military health coverage runs through TRICARE, the health care program for service members and their families. Set up your TRICARE plan early in your PCS. Update your information in DEERS, choose or transfer your plan, and request any referrals before you arrive so there is no gap in care. Full clinic details and specialty services are available at [newengland.tricare.mil](https://newengland.tricare.mil).

## Climate and Things to Do

Newport has a coastal New England climate with four distinct seasons. Summers are warm and humid, typically in the low to mid-80s. Winters bring temperatures in the 30s with occasional nor'easters, moderated by the bay compared with inland New England. The [National Weather Service Boston/Norton office](https://www.weather.gov/box/) covers Newport and publishes local climate normals for planning.

The Newport Mansions, including the Breakers and Marble House operated by the Preservation Society of Newport County, are open for tours year-round. The Cliff Walk is a free 3.5-mile public path tracing the shore between the Atlantic and the mansion lawns.

[Fort Adams State Park](https://riparks.ri.gov/parks/fort-adams-state-park), at the mouth of Newport Harbor, offers panoramic views of Narragansett Bay, fishing, boating, and picnicking, and hosts the annual Newport Jazz Festival and Newport Folk Festival. Narragansett Bay is a sailing destination, with racing and on-water recreation throughout the warmer months.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### What MHA is Naval Station Newport in for BAH?

NAVSTA Newport falls within the Newport, Rhode Island Military Housing Area. Your rate depends on your pay grade and dependent status. Use the official [DoD BAH calculator](https://www.travel.dod.mil/Allowances/Basic-Allowance-for-Housing/BAH-Rate-Lookup/) with your rank and ZIP code to find the current figure.

### Where do military families live near Naval Station Newport?

Common choices are Middletown for the best mix of commute and cost, Portsmouth for more space, and Newport for walkability to base and the waterfront. Jamestown, Tiverton, and Bristol offer additional options, typically with a longer drive and lower prices. On-station housing is also available.

### Is Newport, Rhode Island expensive for military families?

Yes. Newport County is one of the pricier markets in New England. The typical home value in Newport was about $945,429 in April 2026 and about $761,300 in Middletown, according to Zillow. BAH is set to reflect local costs, but comparing options across nearby towns matters. A [VA loan](/blog/how-does-a-0-down-va-loan-work) can help by removing the down payment requirement.

### Which school district will my children attend?

It depends on your address. Newport Public Schools serves the city, Middletown Public Schools covers Middletown, Portsmouth has its own district, and Tiverton School District serves the eastern mainland. Confirm the assigned school for any specific home before you commit.

### Where do I get healthcare at Naval Station Newport?

The Naval Health Clinic New England, Newport at 43 Smith Road provides primary care, specialty referrals, and pharmacy services. Set up TRICARE, update DEERS, and arrange any referrals before you arrive. Full details are at [newengland.tricare.mil](https://newengland.tricare.mil).

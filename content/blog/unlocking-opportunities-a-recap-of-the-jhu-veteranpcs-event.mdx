VeteranPCS recently had the honor of participating in a joint event with the prestigious Johns Hopkins University Carey Business School, dedicated to benefiting veterans and military spouses. The event focused on empowering veterans with knowledge about VA Loans and leveraging them for acquiring multiple investment properties, VA Entitlement, and more.

## Exploring the VA Loan

The heart of the discussion revolved around the VA Loan and its potential for veterans looking to invest in real estate. Attendees gained valuable insights into the intricacies of the VA Loan, learning how to maximize its benefits for acquiring multiple investment properties. The event aimed to demystify the process, providing a roadmap for veterans and military spouses to navigate the world of real estate investment confidently.

## Johns Hopkins University's Perspective

Johns Hopkins University, through its Carey Business School, played a pivotal role in the event by sharing valuable insights into theirMaster of Science in Real Estate and Infrastructure program. Attendees had the opportunity to understand the depth and breadth of the program, gaining a glimpse into the future of real estate education. The university's commitment to helping veterans find meaningful careers in the real estate field was underscored, with a special emphasis on the programs available through theGI Bill and theYellow Ribbon Program.

## GI Bill and Yellow Ribbon Program

Johns Hopkins University is dedicated to supporting veterans in their pursuit of education and career advancement. The GI Bill and theYellow Ribbon Program are two key initiatives that open doors for veterans, providing financial assistance and resources to make education more accessible. These programs enable veterans to invest in their future, creating a pathway to success in the real estate and infrastructure industry.

The JHU VeteranPCS event was a remarkable synergy between Johns Hopkins University's commitment to education and the expertise of VeteranPCS in supporting veterans in the real estate market. By demystifying the complexities of VA Loans, offering insights into advanced real estate programs, and providing avenues for financial assistance, the event showcased the collective effort to empower veterans in their pursuit of meaningful careers and successful real estate endeavors.

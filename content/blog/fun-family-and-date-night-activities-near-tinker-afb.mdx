If you are PCSing to [Tinker Air Force Base](https://www.tinker.af.mil/) in Oklahoma City, you are in for a treat. OKC is a city that punches well above its weight when it comes to family fun, unique date nights, and weekend adventures. Whether you just arrived or have been stationed here for a while, there is always something new to explore. Here is a look at some of the best things to do near Tinker AFB for families, couples, and everyone in between.

## Family-Friendly Fun in Oklahoma City

Oklahoma City offers plenty of options for families with kids of all ages. These spots are easy to reach from Tinker AFB and make for great weekend outings.

**Oklahoma City Zoo and Botanical Garden.** The [OKC Zoo](https://www.okczoo.org/) is one of the top-rated zoos in the region. It features thousands of animals, interactive exhibits, and seasonal events throughout the year. Kids love the Children's Zoo area, while the Sanctuary Asia trail gives everyone a chance to see elephants up close. Plan for a full day because there is a lot of ground to cover.

**Science Museum Oklahoma.** Located just north of downtown, [Science Museum Oklahoma](https://www.sciencemuseumok.org/) is a hands-on wonderland for curious kids and adults. Exhibits cover everything from space exploration to simple machines. The planetarium shows are a highlight, and the outdoor science park is perfect for burning off energy.

**Myriad Botanical Gardens.** Right in the heart of downtown OKC, the [Myriad Botanical Gardens](https://www.myriadgardens.org/) offers beautiful green space, splash pads in the summer, seasonal festivals, and a stunning Crystal Bridge Tropical Conservatory. It is free to walk around the gardens, making it an easy and affordable family outing.

**Frontier City.** For thrill-seeking families, Frontier City is Oklahoma's only theme park. It features roller coasters, water rides, and live entertainment with a Wild West theme. It is a fun day trip, especially during summer months.

## Date Night and Couples Activities Near Tinker AFB

Oklahoma City has a surprisingly vibrant food and entertainment scene. Here are some great date-night options that are easy to get to from Tinker.

**Bricktown Entertainment District.** Bricktown is the go-to spot for date nights in OKC. You will find everything from upscale dining and comedy clubs to a scenic canal with water taxis. Catch a movie at the Harkins Theatre, try a new restaurant, or just stroll along the canal after dinner.

**Paseo Arts District.** If you and your partner enjoy art, the [Paseo Arts District](https://www.thepaseo.org/) is a must. First Friday Art Walks happen monthly, giving you a chance to explore galleries, meet local artists, and enjoy live music. It is a relaxed and unique date night option.

## Outdoor Activities and Recreation

Oklahoma is full of wide-open spaces and outdoor recreation. If you like to stay active, you will not run out of options near Tinker AFB.

**Lake Thunderbird State Park.** Just south of Norman and a short drive from Tinker, Lake Thunderbird offers hiking, fishing, boating, and swimming. It is a great escape from city life and perfect for a family day or a peaceful weekend morning.

**Oklahoma River Trails.** The Oklahoma River trails system runs through the heart of the city and offers miles of paved paths for walking, running, and cycling. The area around the Boathouse District also features kayaking, rowing, and paddleboarding.

**Martin Park Nature Center.** For a quieter outdoor experience, Martin Park offers nature trails through wooded areas with wildlife viewing opportunities. It is a hidden gem and a great spot for a family hike without having to drive far.

## Exploring the Local Food Scene

One of the best parts of a PCS move is discovering a new city's food. OKC's dining scene has exploded in recent years and there is something for every taste.

The Plaza District and Midtown neighborhoods are packed with locally owned restaurants, coffee shops, and bakeries. For authentic Oklahoma flavor, try the barbecue. OKC has several well-known barbecue joints that hold their own against Texas and Kansas City. Do not skip the onion burgers either, a regional specialty that started right here in Oklahoma.

## Making Oklahoma City Home

PCSing to a new city can feel overwhelming, but Oklahoma City is a welcoming and affordable place to live. The cost of living is lower than the national average, and your Basic Allowance for Housing (BAH) tends to go further here than at many other duty stations. Neighborhoods like Midwest City, Del City, and Moore are all popular with Tinker AFB families and offer quick commutes to base.

The military community in OKC is strong, with plenty of support groups, spouse organizations, and community events to help you settle in.

Montana has a deep military history, despite its limited active-duty military presence today. The state’s military community is built around the **Army National Guard and Air National Guard**, with approximately **14,000 soldiers and airmen** serving in various capacities. Currently, **Malmstrom Air Force Base** is the only active-duty military installation in Montana.

### **Malmstrom Air Force Base**

**A Legacy of Strategic Defense**

Malmstrom Air Force Base, located in **Great Falls, Montana**, has played a significant role in **U.S. military operations since World War II**. Its origins date back to the early 1940s when the **Great Falls Chamber of Commerce lobbied for a military presence** in the region. The area was selected as a **training site for heavy bombers**, such as the **B-17 Flying Fortress**, due to its strategic location.

During the **Cold War**, Malmstrom AFB shifted its focus from bomber training to **air defense**, becoming a critical part of the **nation’s nuclear deterrence strategy**. In 1954, the base was renamed **Malmstrom Air Force Base** in honor of **Colonel Einar Axel Malmstrom**, a **World War II prisoner of war** who later died in a plane crash near the base.

#### **Key Functions of Malmstrom AFB**

- **Home to the 341st Missile Wing**, responsible for operating **150 Minuteman III intercontinental ballistic missiles (ICBMs)** as part of the U.S. nuclear defense strategy.
- **One of three U.S. Air Force bases** that maintain **ICBM launch facilities**, ensuring rapid response capabilities.
- **Critical support for missile maintenance, security, and operations personnel**, making it an essential component of national security.
- **A strong presence in the Great Falls community**, offering **economic stability and partnerships with local businesses and organizations**.

Malmstrom AFB continues to be a **key player in the nation’s defense**, maintaining a **state of readiness** to support **strategic deterrence operations**.

For more information, visit the [**Malmstrom Air Force Base website**](https://www.malmstrom.af.mil/).

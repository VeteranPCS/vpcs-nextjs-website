## **Looking to Buy or Sell a Home Near Fort Gordon?**

One of the most frequently asked questions among **Fort Gordon spouses** is, **"What is the best place to live or buy a house off-post near Fort Gordon, Augusta, Georgia?"**

The answer depends on factors such as **family size, lifestyle, income, home budget, commute times, neighborhood style, amenities, and school districts**.

This guide will highlight some of the **best neighborhoods** to consider when relocating to **Fort Gordon**.

## **Top Areas to Consider Near Fort Gordon**

### **Watervale (Martinez - East Columbia County/East of Evans)**

**Location:** Near Savannah River, Stevens Creek Road & Watervale Road intersection

**Description:**

- Established neighborhood from the 1970s with a variety of home styles
- Close to **National Hills Golf Course**, home of _The Masters_
- Mature trees and rolling hills with scenic surroundings
- Features neighborhoods such as Cambridge, The Highlands, Gloucester, and Stevens Pointe
- Some communities offer a **clubhouse, pool, and playground**

**Price Range:** $200,000s - $600,000+

**Pros:**

- Older homes with larger yards and mature trees
- Quick freeway access and central location
- Close to downtown Augusta, riverwalk, and Evans
- Great for renting out during _The Masters_ tournament
- Nearby **Savannah Rapids Park & Augusta Canal Historic Trail**

**Commute to Fort Gordon:** 20-25 minutes

**Nearby Neighborhoods:**

- Hunters Ridge
- Hampstead
- Bakers Ferry

### **Canterbury Farms (Northwest of Grovetown Center)**

**Location:** Near Augusta Area, Chamblin Road & Canterbury Farms Parkway

**Description:**

- Newer community with homes built in the 2000s and beyond
- Features **townhomes and single-family homes**
- Community amenities include a **pool, clubhouse, and playground**
- Access to **Euchee Creek Trail**, a long paved trail

**Price Range:** $300,000+

**Pros:**

- Newer homes with modern architecture
- Convenient access to **Fort Gordon**
- Active **commercial development** nearby
- Great investment potential for rental property

**Commute to Fort Gordon:** 12-20 minutes

**Nearby Neighborhoods:**

- Kelarie
- Hidden Creek

### **Whispering Pines (Northwest of Evans Towne Center)**

**Location:** Close to Golf Course, William Few Parkway & Georgia Pine Trail

**Description:**

- **New construction community** with **large homes**
- Still expanding, offering various builders and styles
- Features a **community pool and clubhouse**

**Price Range:** $400,000+

**Pros:**

- Newer construction homes with 4+ bedrooms
- Quiet, **suburban atmosphere with modern amenities**
- Quick access to **Publix, restaurants, and coffee shops**

**Commute to Fort Gordon:** 25-30 minutes

**Nearby Neighborhoods:**

- Highland Lakes
- Pinebluff

### **Crawford Creek (Evans/Grovetown, Southwest of Evans Towne Center)**

**Location:** Hereford Farm Road & Columbia Road

**Description:**

- Beautiful neighborhood with **green spaces** and a **resort-style pool**
- Homes vary in size, some include **basements**
- Features a **lap pool, community clubhouse, and walking trails**

**Price Range:** $300,000s - $700,000s+

**Pros:**

- Well-established neighborhood with newer homes
- Great investment potential
- **Central location** near Evans and Fort Gordon

**Commute to Fort Gordon:** 20 minutes

**Nearby Neighborhoods:**

- Tudor Branch

### **Haynes Station (West Augusta, Across from Fort Gordon)**

**Location:** Haynes Station Drive & Gordon Highway

**Description:**

- **Newer development directly across from Fort Gordon**
- Large homes with access to a **community pool and clubhouse**
- Close to **new commercial developments**

**Price Range:** $200,000s - $300,000s

**Pros:**

- **Fastest commute to Fort Gordon** (5-10 minutes)
- Convenient access to **grocery stores, restaurants, and amenities**
- Good potential for rental income

**Commute to Fort Gordon:** 5-10 minutes

**Nearby Neighborhoods:**

- Captains Corner

### **Lake Aumond/Forest Hills/Summerville (West Augusta)**

**Location:** Near National Hills Golf Course

**Description:**

- **Historic homes** mixed with **modern remodels**
- Close to **Augusta University and the medical district**
- Access to several **golf courses and downtown Augusta**

**Price Range:** $200,000 - $800,000+

**Pros:**

- Unique **historic architecture**
- **Short commute to downtown Augusta**
- Great investment for **renting during The Masters**

**Commute to Fort Gordon:** 15-25 minutes

**Nearby Neighborhoods:**

- National Hills

### **Walnut Hill/Camelot (Heart of Evans)**

**Location:** Country Place Lane & Washington Road

**Description:**

- **Quiet, established neighborhood** with large lots
- Features older homes and **ranch-style homes**
- Walkable access to **shops, restaurants, and parks** in Evans

**Price Range:** $200,000s - $500,000s

**Pros:**

- Mature trees and **spacious yards**
- **Quick access to Evans Towne Center**
- Family-friendly community

**Commute to Fort Gordon:** 20-30 minutes

**Nearby Neighborhoods:**

- Woodbridge

## **Looking to Buy or Sell a Home Near Eglin AFB?**

Welcome to **Northwest Florida**—home to **Eglin Air Force Base, Hurlburt Field, Duke Field, and the 7th Special Forces Group**. With multiple military installations in close proximity, you’ll find **a strong military community, excellent housing options, and top-rated schools**.

If you're **PCSing to Eglin AFB or nearby**, this guide will help you explore the **best neighborhoods and communities** for military families.

## **Top Places to Live Near Eglin AFB**

### **Niceville, FL – The Top Choice for Military Families**

**Best for:** Eglin AFB personnel

If you’re stationed at **Eglin AFB**, **Niceville, FL** is an **ideal location** with **top-rated schools, safe neighborhoods, and a strong military presence**.

**Why Choose Niceville?**

- **Close to the East Gate of Eglin AFB**
- **Convenient to Northwest Florida Regional Airport & Northwest Florida State College**
- **Near Destin’s world-class beaches & entertainment**
- **15-20 minute commute to Eglin AFB**

### **Popular Neighborhoods in Niceville:**

- Swift Creek
- Deer Moss Creek
- Woodridge
- Osprey Ridge
- Blue Water Bay

## **Crestview – A Great Alternative**

**Best for:** Duke Field, 7th Special Forces Group, and Eglin AFB personnel

For those stationed at **Duke Field or the 7th Special Forces Group**, **Crestview, FL** is another **excellent choice**. It offers **affordable housing, a great school system, and easy access to Interstate-10**.

**Why Choose Crestview?**

- **Lower home prices compared to Niceville**
- **More shopping & dining options**
- **15-20 minute commute to Duke Field & 7th SFG**
- **30-40 minute commute to Eglin AFB**

### **Popular Neighborhoods in Crestview:**

- Ridgeway Landing
- Silvercrest
- Colton Blaine
- Patriot Ridge
- Timberland Ridge

## **Hurlburt Field – A Hidden Gem**

**Best for:** Air Force Special Operations personnel

As the **home of Air Force Special Operations**, **Hurlburt Field** is **one of the best-kept secrets in the Air Force**. If you’re stationed here, you’ll love the **proximity to white-sand beaches, top schools, and great housing options**.

**Best Areas for Hurlburt Field Personnel:**

- **Navarre** – Holly-by-the-Sea
- **Mary Esther** – Mary Esther Manor
- **Fort Walton Beach** – Ferry Park, Kenwood, Seabreeze

**Most of these locations are within a 30-minute commute to Hurlburt Field.**

## Schools, Commutes, and Cost of Living near Eglin AFB

When you pick a neighborhood here, three things usually decide it: the schools, the drive to your gate, and what your housing dollars buy. Here is a plain look at each one.

### Schools by County

Eglin AFB and Hurlburt Field sit in **Okaloosa County**, while Navarre sits next door in **Santa Rosa County**. Each county runs its own school district, so the school your child attends depends on which side of the county line you settle.

In Okaloosa County, Niceville is the popular choice for families. Niceville Senior High School holds a [7 out of 10 rating on GreatSchools](https://www.greatschools.org/florida/niceville/1953-Niceville-Senior-High-School/) and serves about 2,000 students in grades 9 through 12, making it one of 19 high schools in the [Okaloosa County School District](https://www.greatschools.org/florida/fort-walton-beach/okaloosa-county-school-district/). If you land in Navarre, your children attend Santa Rosa County schools. Navarre High School carries a [5 out of 10 GreatSchools rating](https://www.greatschools.org/florida/navarre/2765-Navarre-High-School/), which the site lists as average for Florida. Ratings shift each year, so check the current score for the exact school your address feeds into before you commit.

### Commute Times to the Gates

Your daily drive matters more than almost anything else. From Niceville, most families reach the East Gate of Eglin AFB in about 15 to 20 minutes. Crestview sits farther north near Interstate 10, so it is a short hop to Duke Field and the 7th Special Forces Group but a 30 to 40 minute drive to Eglin's main area. Navarre, Mary Esther, and Fort Walton Beach all sit within roughly half an hour of Hurlburt Field. Drive your likely route during morning rush hour before you sign anything, since summer beach traffic on Highway 98 can stretch those times.

### Housing Costs and BAH

Prices vary by town. In Crestview, Zillow reports a typical home value of about $291,000 as of mid-2026, a figure that has held nearly flat over the past year. That is a [typical home value, not a median sale price](https://www.zillow.com/home-values/24265/crestview-fl/), so treat it as a starting point rather than an offer. Niceville and the beach towns tend to run higher.

Your housing budget is shaped by your **Basic Allowance for Housing (BAH)** — the monthly amount the military pays toward rent or a mortgage based on your rank, your dependents, and your duty station's zip code. Eglin and Hurlburt fall in the same Military Housing Area, so rates are tied to this region rather than to one town. Rates change every January, so look up your current amount with the official [BAH calculator at the Defense Travel Management Office](https://www.travel.dod.mil/Allowances/Basic-Allowance-for-Housing/) before you set a price range.

### Room to Live Near a Working Base

It helps to know what you are moving next to. Eglin is [the U.S. Air Force's largest installation](https://www.eglin.af.mil/About-Us/Fact-Sheets/Display/Article/390964/eglin-air-force-base-history/), built on land that was once the Choctawhatchee National Forest, with the 96th Test Wing handling weapons testing across its wide ranges. That size is part of why the surrounding towns stay spread out and green, with Destin's beaches and Northwest Florida State College both an easy drive from Niceville.
